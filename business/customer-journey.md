# customer-journey.md  

**Product:** Founder‑Launchpad – a no‑code/low‑code platform that lets non‑technical founders spin up, test, and iterate on a SaaS or marketplace idea in days instead of months.  

---  

## 1. AWARE  

| Element | Detail |
|---|---|
| **Trigger event** | Founder reads a blog post, listens to a podcast, or sees a social‑media ad about “launching a startup without writing code”. |
| **Friction points** | • Over‑whelmed by the sheer number of “no‑code” tools (Webflow, Bubble, Glide, etc.).<br>• Skepticism: “Can a platform really replace a dev team?”. |
| **User emotions** | Curious → Cautiously optimistic, but also anxious about wasting time/money. |
| **Opportunities to delight** | • A 30‑second explainer video that shows a real founder building an MVP in 3 hours.<br>• A “Founder‑Fit Quiz” that instantly tells the user if their idea matches the platform’s strengths. |
| **Metric** | **Impression‑to‑Click Rate (CTR)** – % of ad/blog viewers who click the “Learn More” link. Target: **≥ 3 %** within the first month. |

---  

## 2. CONSIDER  

| Element | Detail |
|---|---|
| **Trigger event** | User lands on the Founder‑Launchpad homepage after clicking the ad/quiz result. |
| **Friction points** | • Unclear pricing & ROI.<br>• Fear of lock‑in or hidden technical debt.<br>• Need to validate that the platform supports their specific domain (e.g., SaaS, marketplace, mobile‑first). |
| **User emotions** | Analytical, weighing options; still hopeful but increasingly cautious. |
| **Opportunities to delight** | • Interactive pricing calculator that projects **time‑to‑revenue** vs. hiring a dev team.<br>• Live “Idea‑Fit Demo” where the user selects a template and sees a pre‑populated mock‑up instantly.<br>• Social proof carousel with short video testimonials from non‑technical founders who raised seed rounds after using the platform. |
| **Metric** | **Qualified‑Lead Rate** – % of visitors who complete the pricing calculator or request a demo. Target: **≥ 12 %**. |

---  

## 3. TRY  

| Element | Detail |
|---|---|
| **Trigger event** | Founder signs up for the **Free 7‑day trial** (no credit card required). |
| **Friction points** | • Onboarding complexity – too many steps before building anything.<br>• Limited template library may feel restrictive.<br>• Uncertainty about data ownership & export. |
| **User emotions** | Excited to build, but quickly becomes frustrated if the UI is unintuitive. |
| **Opportunities to delight** | • Guided “Launch‑in‑1‑Hour” wizard that walks the user through creating a landing page, a sign‑up form, and a basic workflow.<br>• Real‑time chat assistance with a **Founder‑Coach Bot** that suggests next steps based on the user’s progress.<br>• One‑click export of the project to a GitHub repo for later hand‑off. |
| **Metric** | **Activation Rate** – % of trial users who publish a live MVP (public URL) within 48 h. Target: **≥ 35 %**. |

---  

## 4. ADOPT  

| Element | Detail |
|---|---|
| **Trigger event** | Founder converts to a paid plan after seeing early traction (sign‑ups, feedback) on the MVP. |
| **Friction points** | • Decision on which paid tier (features, scaling, team seats).<br>• Need for integrations (payment, email, analytics).<br>• Concerns about support responsiveness. |
| **User emotions** | Confident about the idea, but nervous about scaling and cost. |
| **Opportunities to delight** | • Dedicated **Customer Success Manager** for the first 30 days (personal onboarding call, integration checklist).<br>• “Growth Pack” add‑ons (A/B testing, SEO boost, automated onboarding emails) offered at a discounted rate for the first month.<br>• Community‑only “Founder‑Launchpad Slack” where users share launch metrics and get peer feedback. |
| **Metric** | **Conversion Rate (Trial → Paid)** – % of trial users who upgrade within 14 days. Target: **≥ 22 %**. |

---  

## 5. EXPAND  

| Element | Detail |
|---|---|
| **Trigger event** | Founder reaches a milestone (e.g., 1,000 users, $5k MRR) and looks to add features or scale infrastructure. |
| **Friction points** | • Understanding the value of higher‑tier plans (team seats, custom domains, white‑label).<br>• Migration concerns if moving from a low‑tier to enterprise‑grade resources. |
| **User emotions** | Proud of progress, eager to grow, but wary of “outgrowing” the platform. |
| **Opportunities to delight** | • **Expansion Playbook**: a PDF + video series showing how to add advanced features (role‑based access, multi‑currency payments, API extensions).<br>• Early‑access beta program for upcoming AI‑assisted product features (e.g., auto‑generated copy, predictive analytics).<br>• Loyalty discount: 15 % off the next tier upgrade if done within 30 days of hitting a predefined metric. |
| **Metric** **Expansion Revenue Rate** – % of existing customers who upgrade to a higher tier or purchase add‑ons within 90 days of hitting a growth milestone. Target: **≥ 18 %**. |

---  

### Summary of Key Metrics  

| Phase | Primary Metric | Target |
|---|---|---|
| Aware | Impression‑to‑Click Rate (CTR) | ≥ 3 % |
| Consider | Qualified‑Lead Rate | ≥ 12 % |
| Try | Activation Rate (MVP published) | ≥ 35 % |
| Adopt | Trial → Paid Conversion | ≥ 22 % |
| Expand | Expansion Revenue Rate | ≥ 18 % |

Tracking these metrics in the BRAIN dashboard will surface drop‑off points early, allowing rapid iteration on onboarding flows, pricing communication, and growth‑stage support.
