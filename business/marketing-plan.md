| **Customer Acquisition Cost (CAC)** | **≤ $27** | N/A | Blend of paid, organic, partner channels. |
| **Conversion Rate (Trial → Paid)** | **5 %** | N/A | Industry benchmark for no‑code SaaS platforms. |
| **Net Promoter Score (NPS)** | **≥ 45** | N/A | Target for strong product‑market fit. |
| **Churn (first 90 days)** | **≤ 8 %** | N/A | Early‑stage SaaS benchmark. |
