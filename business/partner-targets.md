# Partner Targets

## Integration Roadmap

### Phase 1: Core Foundational Integrations (0-3 months)
1. **Stripe**
   - **Rationale**: Essential for payment processing and subscription management.
   - **Free-tier limits**: 1% transaction fee + $0.30 per successful card charge.
   - **Integration effort**: S
   - **Value-add**: Enables founders to monetize their ideas immediately.

2. **SendGrid**
   - **Rationale**: Critical for email communication and marketing.
   - **Free-tier limits**: 100 emails/day.
   - **Integration effort**: S
   - **Value-add**: Allows founders to engage with their users via email campaigns.

3. **AWS S3**
   - **Rationale**: Necessary for file storage and management.
   - **Free-tier limits**: 5GB standard storage, 20,000 GET requests, 2,000 PUT requests.
   - **Integration effort**: M
   - **Value-add**: Provides scalable storage for user-generated content.

### Phase 2: Enhanced Functionality (3-6 months)
4. **Zapier**
   - **Rationale**: Facilitates automation and integration with other SaaS tools.
   - **Free-tier limits**: 100 tasks/month.
   - **Integration effort**: M
   - **Value-add**: Enables founders to automate workflows and connect with other tools.

5. **Twilio**
   - **Rationale**: Essential for SMS and voice communication.
   - **Free-tier limits**: $15 credit for first month, then pay-as-you-go.
   - **Integration effort**: M
   - **Value-add**: Allows founders to send SMS notifications and make voice calls.

### Phase 3: Advanced Features (6-12 months)
6. **Google Analytics**
   - **Rationale**: Provides insights into user behavior and website performance.
   - **Free-tier limits**: Unlimited data collection and reporting.
   - **Integration effort**: S
   - **Value-add**: Helps founders track and analyze user engagement.

7. **Intercom**
   - **Rationale**: Enhances customer support and engagement.
   - **Free-tier limits**: Up to 500 contacts.
   - **Integration effort**: M
   - **Value-add**: Enables founders to provide personalized support and engage with users.

8. **HubSpot**
   - **Rationale**: Comprehensive CRM and marketing automation.
   - **Free-tier limits**: Up to 1,000,000 contacts, 2,000 emails/month.
   - **Integration effort**: L
   - **Value-add**: Provides a full suite of tools for customer relationship management and marketing.

## Affiliate/Revenue-Share Opportunities
- **Stripe**: Offers revenue-share for successful referrals.
- **SendGrid**: Provides affiliate program for email marketing services.
- **Zapier**: Has an affiliate program for automation tools.
- **Twilio**: Offers revenue-share for successful referrals.
- **HubSpot**: Provides affiliate program for CRM and marketing tools.
